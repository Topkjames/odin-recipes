Beef Burger Recipe

This classic beef burger recipe is perfect for family dinners and special occasions. Juicy beef patties, fresh vegetables, and melted cheese make this a crowd-pleaser!

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 beef patties (8 oz each)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar or American)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions (optional)
  • Burger sauce or ketchup (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the beef patties with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness.
  4. Add a slice of cheese on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toasted buns are ready to be assembled with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and sauce if desired.
