Pizza Recipe

This classic pizza recipe is perfect for family dinners and special occasions. Topped with your favorite ingredients, it's a crowd-pleaser!

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Sprinkle the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  5. Add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
