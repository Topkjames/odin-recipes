Lasagna Recipe

This classic lasagna recipe is perfect for family dinners and special occasions. Layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, it's a crowd-pleaser!

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and add minced garlic.
  4. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, and seasonings in a bowl.
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses on top.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
